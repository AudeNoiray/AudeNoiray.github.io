Following the [Journées de la Phonétique Clinique in Sète](https://jpc2025.sciencesconf.org), I collaborated on a podcast from la [SURO](https://surorthophonie.com). In this podcast, I talk about 3 collaborative studies carried on (1) vocal and respiratory development in babies, (2) the development of spoken and reading fluency in children and (3) post stroke aphasia. The podcast is available on [spotify](https://open.spotify.com/episode/4upwaUoyzT3QgFx1invj0K?si=8886f4e48b9f4336).
